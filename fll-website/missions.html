Mission 00 Equipment Inspection

Before the match begins, the referee inspects your robot. Everything must fit in one Launch area and be under the height limit.

Mission 01 The Great Escape

You are in a mine cart being chased by bad guys! Push the mine cart through the arch so it rests on the other side — and keep it on the track.

Mine cart is through the arch 10 pts
Mine cart is still on the track, not touching the mat 10 pts
Max possible20 pts
📋 Official Q&A
The instructions say "push." Must it be pushed, or can it be pulled or the track lifted to move the mine cart?
You can accomplish the mission in any legal way.
Mission 02 Restore the Cave

The cave is about to collapse! Place a boulder inside the cave without knocking the walls over. Hit the orange circle for bonus points.

Boulder is inside the cave and walls are still up 20 pts
Bonus: Boulder is on the orange circle 10 pts
Max possible30 pts
📋 Official Q&A
Are there any points if the boulder is inside the cave, but the walls are knocked down?
No, the walls must be up to score points.

Mission 10 Claim Territory

Many archaeologists are competing for dig sites! Place markers on any green panel or in the grey divot to claim your territory. Markers cannot touch the mat.

Each marker placed on a dig site (green panel or grey divot), not touching mat 10 pts ea.
Max possible (2 markers, one per dig site)20 pts
📋 Official Q&A
Can we put all the site markers on one dig site (e.g., all 3 on one green dig site spot)?
Only two markers will count, and there needs to be one at each dig site.
Does the term "grey divot" refer to the small recessed/sunken (trapezoid-shaped) area of the grey model?
Yes — the grey divot does refer to the recessed area on the grey model.
Does the marker on the green divot have to be standing, or can it tip over and still count?
As long as it is completely on the divot, it will count.
